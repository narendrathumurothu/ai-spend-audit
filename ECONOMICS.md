# ECONOMICS

## Lead economics

Assumption: a converted lead is worth $3,500 to Credex.
- Average credit deal size: $20,000
- Gross margin: 35%
- Sales cycle win rate from qualified lead: 20%
- Lead value = $20,000 * 35% * 20% = $1,400
- Add upsell and retention lift to get a blended LTV of about $3,500.

## CAC by channel

- Hacker News / X thread: 100 audits from 0 paid spend, 20 leads, 5 consultations. CAC is effectively $0 for first 100 users.
- Slack / community outreach: 2–3 hours of founder outreach per channel; assume 10 warm audits per 5 hours. Cost is founder time, not ad spend.
- Organic SEO / content: slow ramp but low marginal cost once the landing page ranks.

## Conversion funnel assumptions

- Audit completed → lead captured: 20%
- Lead captured → consultation booked: 25%
- Consultation booked → credit purchase: 20%

If 1,000 visitors complete audits:
- 1,000 audits
- 200 leads
- 50 consultations
- 10 credit purchases
- ARR impact = 10 * $20,000 * 35% = $70,000

## What must be true for $1M ARR in 18 months

- The tool needs 5,000 qualified audit completions per month by month 18.
- Conversion to lead should be at least 15–20%.
- Consultation booking should be 20–25% of leads.
- Credit purchase rate should be 15–20% of consultations.

That path is reasonable if the free audit becomes a strong lead magnet into Credex’s existing enterprise credit funnel.
