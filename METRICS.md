# Metrics

## North Star metric

**Qualified AI spend audits completed.**

This metric captures the core value of the tool: founders and engineering leaders completing the audit and seeing their savings potential.

## Input metrics

1. **Lead capture rate** — percentage of completed audits that submit an email.
2. **High-savings rate** — percentage of audits with >$500/mo savings, which is the threshold for Credex consultation promotion.
3. **Shareable link conversions** — number of public audit URLs opened by a second viewer.

## Instrument first

- Audit completion events
- Savings summary displayed
- Lead capture submissions
- High-savings CTA interactions
- Share page visits

## Pivot trigger

If fewer than **15%** of completed audits convert to an email capture after the first 1,000 audits, I would pivot the onboarding flow or the messaging to improve trust and perceived value.
